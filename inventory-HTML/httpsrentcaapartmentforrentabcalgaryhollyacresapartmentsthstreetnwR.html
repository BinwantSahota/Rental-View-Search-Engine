Holly Acres Apartments

Visit our Website: >

Residents enjoy the park-like setting in the sought-after community of Varsity in the heart of Calgary’s NW. Building amenities include:

 

  • Holly Acres is pet-friendly to cats and small/medium dogs, with river valley walking paths close by to keep your furry friends happy
  • Meet your fitness goals with an on-site fitness centre equipped with treadmill, elliptical, and free weights
  • An on-site laundry room operated by Coinamatic ensures you get your washing done conveniently and efficiently
  • Make the most of summer with outdoor gathering spaces lined with mature trees, and picnic tables
  • Powered surface parking is available for tenants at a monthly rate
  • Secure entry and on-site management help to keep the building secure and safe for residents
  • The main tower features an elevator to help make moving in a breeze
