Continental Towers

Continental Towers is located near Downtown Shopping Centre, CO-OP and Safeway and close to numerous restaurants, parks, schools and colleges. They are only steps away from LRT, various major bus stops, and only minutes away from Crowchild, Bow Trail and McLeod Trail.

Continental Towers offers sensational city and mountain views with a beautifully maintained courtyard. The suites have been updated and contain large balconies. The building is a pet-free community. Continental Towers offers in-suite storage and parking is available for tenants and visitors at impark at an additional cost.
