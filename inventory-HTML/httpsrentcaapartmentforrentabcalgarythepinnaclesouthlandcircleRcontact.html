The Pinnacle

If you're looking for a home with distinction - you'll find it at The Pinnacle. A prestigious address, this rental community measures up to expectations with spacious, modern suites, large balconies, five full-sized appliances including dishwasher and in-suite laundry, a fitness centre, and The Pinnacle Club. Stylish, contemporary interiors are equaled by the incredible views - clear to the Majestic Rockies and great cityscape panoramas, many suites overlooking the clear waters of the Glenmore Reservoir.

Nestled into a quiet side street, The Pinnacle enjoys an idyllic tranquility, with added extensive urban convenience. Close to public transit and the C-Train, it’s also easy to access Macleod Trail and Anderson Road, making it a quick trip across the city and beyond. Close to downtown, this is a great rental community to call home while enjoying the best that the city has to offer. Residents have a great variety of amenities within walking distance, from cafés and restaurants to banks, groceries, parks, businesses and more, making The Pinnacle a perfect choice to rent for upscale living.
