Located in the quiet neighborhood of Lynnwood nestled on the ridge overlooking Beaverdam Flats and the Bow River, this location offers gorgeous views and lots of walking paths to get you back to nature.

  • A playground in the central greenspace will help to get the kids outside for some fresh air without going far
  • An on-site laundry room operated by Coinamatic ensures you get your washing done conveniently and efficiently
  • Outdoor parking stalls are available for tenants at a monthly rate
  • Plenty of visitor parking for when your friends or family come to visit
  • Secure entry and on-site management help to keep the building secure and safe for residents
